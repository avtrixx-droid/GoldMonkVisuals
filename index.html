About Me

Hi, I’m Rumit Khade.

I’m a Pune-based photographer and the creative eye behind GoldMonkVisuals, serving people and brands across Pune, Mumbai, and beyond. I enjoy photographing a wide range of subjects, from portraits and events to lifestyle and candid moments, but sports photography is where my passion truly lies.

Capturing the intensity of competition, the raw emotions of players, and those split-second moments that define the game is what excites me the most. Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to cover tournaments such as the DY Patil T20 League, TRPS Corporate Cricket League, and several local and club cricket tournaments, documenting the journey of teams and athletes through powerful visuals.

My goal is simple: to create images that allow players, teams, and fans to relive the energy and emotion of the game long after the final ball is bowled.
